## The Origin of Power: The Butterfly Curse & The Altered Gene Complex  
## *Power in our universe is not a gift. It is a scar left by history—a genetic echo of a single, catastrophic mistake. To understand the altered, one must first understand the curse that birthed them.*  
  
The source of nearly all inherent superhuman ability on Earth is the **Altered Gene Complex (AGC)**, a latent genetic trait that, when activated, grants extraordinary powers.  
  
**The Butterfly Curse**  
The AGC was not a natural evolution. It was created in the 16th century when the powerful witch **Minka Myst** cast the **Butterfly Curse** upon the leader of the Voraxn clan. The curse was intended as a punishment, but its magical energies interacted with an ancient cosmic artifact known as the **Vira Crucible**. A nearby craftsman, **Henry Hodge**, was exposed to this volatile fusion of magic and cosmic energy, and his DNA was irrevocably rewritten.  
He became the first carrier of the AGC, a complex and adaptive genetic blueprint that would be passed down through his lineage and, over centuries, spread throughout the global population.  
**AGC Activation**  
The AGC remains dormant until triggered by a specific catalyst. Activation methods include:  
* **Extreme Trauma:** Intense physical or emotional stress can force a latent AGC to manifest.  
* **Energy Exposure:** Contact with rare materials (like Nechronium), cosmic radiation, or mystical artifacts can trigger activation.  
* **Scientific Intervention:** Organizations like the GEB have developed serums (**APEX**, **NeuroGenetics**) and procedures (**The Cognitive Chamber**) to forcibly and often brutally activate the AGC in subjects.  
The nature of the manifested power is influenced by the individual's unique genetic makeup and the specific circumstances of their activation, resulting in a vast spectrum of abilities.  
  
  
**1. The Tragedy of Minka Myst**  
The story of the Altered Gene Complex begins not in a sterile laboratory, but in the fog-choked depths of the 16th-century Hushwood Forest. It was a time of superstition, where the veil between the mundane and the mystical was thin and frayed. Here lived the Myst coven, a lineage of witches bound to the natural world, led by the formidable matriarch, **Minka Myst**. She was a guardian of balance, her magic rooted in the earth's ancient rhythms.  
Her sworn enemies were the Voraxn, a predatory clan of humanoid hunters who saw the forest not as a sanctuary to be protected, but as a resource to be conquered. Their leader, driven by an insatiable hunger for power, sought to dominate the forest's mystical energies, threatening to shatter the delicate harmony Minka had sworn her life to maintain.  
In a desperate, final confrontation, Minka sought to end the conflict permanently. She prepared a ritual of binding—the **Butterfly Curse**—a hex designed to attach itself to the Voraxn leader's life force and ensure his every ambition would crumble to ruin. But the ritual was interrupted. In the heat of the magical torrent, the Voraxn leader shattered a sacred artifact hidden in the grove: the **Vira Crucible**, a relic of unknown cosmic origin that pulsed with primordial energy.  
The curse, unmoored from its intended target, fused with the cosmic power erupting from the Crucible. The resulting explosion was not of fire, but of pure, reality-altering magic. It was a silent, violet blast that washed over the forest, rewriting the very laws of nature in an instant. The curse did not vanish; it became something new. Something infectious.  
  
**2. The Genesis of the First Altered**  
Miles away, a simple craftsman named **Henry Hodge** was carving a wooden effigy at the forest's edge. When the violet wave of energy washed over him, he felt nothing but a sudden chill. He went home, unaware that the fused curse had found a host. He was the first.  
The transformation was not immediate. It was a slow, biological horror. Over weeks, Henry's body began to change. His skin developed an unnatural resilience; his strength grew beyond human limits. Then came the true terror: his son, **Alastor Hodge**, was born with eyes that glowed with a faint, silvery light. The curse had not just altered Henry; it had become a **"Genetic Echo,"** a heritable blueprint that would lie dormant in his bloodline for all time.  
This blueprint became known, centuries later, as the **Altered Gene Complex (AGC)**. It was a latent, adaptive structure embedded in the DNA of Hodge's descendants and, through the slow march of generations, a significant portion of the human population. The curse had become a permanent part of humanity's potential.  
**Field Report Excerpt - Scribe of the Myst Coven, est. 1588:** *"The air itself feels wrong. The curse Minka unleashed did not die with the Voraxn. It lingers, a poison in the blood of the innocent. We have seen the Hodge child. His eyes hold a light that is not of this world. This is not a blessing. It is a plague waiting for its season."*  
  
**3. Scientific Rediscovery of the AGC**  
For centuries, the AGC was the source of myths, legends, and folklore—tales of individuals with inexplicable gifts, often persecuted as witches or demons. It was not until the 20th century that modern science began to unravel the mystery. Clandestine organizations like **D.A.R.K.** and the **Genetic Enhancement Bureau (GEB)**, while searching for the key to creating super-soldiers, stumbled upon a recurring anomaly in the human genome.  
They found a complex, non-coding genetic sequence that remained inert in most subjects but showed volatile potential under specific stimuli. They named it the Altered Gene Complex. Early theories dismissed it as a junk mutation, but a few brilliant, and morally flexible, minds saw its true nature.  
**From the White Paper "The Reconfiguration Event: A New Paradigm in Human Potential" - Dr. Julian Sable, GEB Director:** *"The AGC is not a mutation; it is a reconfiguration event waiting for a key. It does not add something new to the human genome so much as it unlocks what has been sleeping there for centuries. It is an epigenetic switch of terrifying and beautiful potential. Our task is not to fear it, but to find the key and, if necessary, to forge it."*  
  
**4. Triggers of Activation**  
The AGC remains dormant until a specific catalyst forces it to manifest. The nature of the trigger often influences the type of power that emerges, turning an individual's life into a crucible of pain, fear, or violent transformation.  
* **Trauma-Triggered Activation:** The most common and unpredictable catalyst. Intense physical injury, profound psychological distress, or near-death experiences can cause a violent, spontaneous AGC activation.  
    * **Timeline:** Manifestation is often instantaneous and explosive.  
    * **Side Effects:** Powers are typically unstable, directly linked to the emotional state of the user. The individual often suffers from PTSD, with their trauma becoming a literal trigger for their abilities.  
    * **Case Example (Tyler Wilder - "Cartridge"):** Subjected to the GEB's brutal Cognitive Chamber, the psychological trauma forced the activation of his AGC, granting him perfect marksmanship and allowing the integration of a weapon system into his arm. His focus is a direct result of his reconditioned trauma response.  
* **Environmental Exposure:** Contact with exotic materials or energies can activate the AGC by acting as a "key" to its latent structure.  
    * **Timeline:** Activation can be gradual or immediate, depending on the intensity and duration of the exposure.  
    * **Side Effects:** Powers are often thematically linked to the exposure source. The body may suffer from radiation sickness, cellular decay, or an ongoing dependency on the environmental source to maintain power stability.  
    * **Case Example (The Tanaka Triplets):** Exposure to a blast of temporal, quantum, and spectral energies from a sabotaged Velocity Enhancer activated the latent AGC in all three siblings, granting them interconnected powers over those very forces.  
* **Forced Activation:** Clandestine organizations have perfected methods of artificially triggering the AGC. This is the most controlled, and most horrific, form of activation.  
    * **Timeline:** The process is scientifically managed, often taking days or weeks in controlled laboratory settings.  
    * **Methods:** Includes injection of proprietary serums like **NeuroGenetics**, prolonged exposure to conditioning devices like the **Cognitive Chamber**, or direct psionic manipulation.  
    * **Side Effects:** While the resulting powers are often more stable, the psychological cost is immense. Subjects are frequently brainwashed, their identities erased, and their loyalty programmed. They become living weapons, devoid of free will.  
    * **Case Example (Tiffany Wilder):** Her powerful telepathic and psychokinetic abilities were forcibly awakened and amplified in the GEB's Cognitive Chamber. The process shattered her psyche, turning her into a vengeful and immensely powerful villain.  
* **Inherited Activation:** In rare cases, the AGC can become so potent within a bloodline that it begins to activate spontaneously in younger generations, often during puberty. This suggests a form of "familial resonance" where the gene complex becomes more dominant over time.  
  
**5. AGC Typing & Variability**  
Not all AGC manifestations are equal. UMED and the GEB have developed a classification system to categorize the nature of an altered human's abilities. This typing often determines their societal status, from "safe" and employable to "wild" and hunted.  
* **AGC-A (Alteredhuman):** The baseline designation for any individual with an activated AGC.  
* **AGC-P (Psychic):** Abilities related to the mind, such as telepathy, telekinesis, or precognition. Often considered the most dangerous and unstable type. (e.g., Tiffany Wilder).  
* **AGC-E (Elemental):** The ability to manipulate natural forces like fire, water, or electricity.  
* **AGC-X (Exotic/Unclassified):** Powers that defy conventional classification, such as reality manipulation, biological mimicry, or temporal distortion.  
The side effects of activation are as varied as the powers themselves. Many altered individuals suffer from chronic pain, metabolic instability, or severe psychological strain. Public perception is deeply divided, with many fearing "wild" or uncontrolled activations, leading to prejudice and discriminatory laws.  
**Psychological Profile Excerpt - UMED File #771-B (Subject: Standstill):** *"Subject Silas Rowan's temporal stasis ability appears to be a direct psychological manifestation of his past as a conflict photographer. He 'freezes' moments of violence, just as he once did with a camera. However, this activation has come at a cost. His perception of time is fracturing. He reports moments of dissociation where he perceives the world as a series of still frames. The curse has given him the power he always wanted, but it is also unmaking him from the inside out."*  
  
  
**6. The Curse in Society**  
The existence of the AGC has irrevocably scarred the social and political landscape.  
* **Legislation & Control:** Governments worldwide have enacted "Altered Registration Acts," forcing individuals with known AGC traits to register with the state. This has created an underclass of citizens, tracked and monitored, their rights often curtailed.  
* **Fear of "The Bloom":** Spontaneous, public activation events, known colloquially as "Blooms," are a source of constant public terror. A teenager losing control at a high school prom or a stressed office worker leveling a city block has fueled widespread fear and paranoia.  
* **Cults and Exploitation:** The mystical origins of the AGC have given rise to cults who worship the Butterfly Curse as a divine event. Others, like the brokers on the Power Exchange, see AGC-carriers as little more than cattle to be harvested for their abilities.  
  
**7. The Gene Sanctity’s Position**  
The most extreme reaction to the AGC comes from the **Gene Sanctity**, a global, militant organization that views the Altered Gene Complex as a genetic plague. They see the Butterfly Curse not as an evolution, but as a corruption of the human form.  
**From "The Supreme Architect's First Edict":** *"Humanity was not meant to be a vessel for this chaotic magic. The AGC is a stain upon our species, a perversion of our natural path. We are not its guardians; we are its purifiers. We will hunt every carrier, every echo of the curse, and we will cleanse our genome with fire and with faith."*  
The Gene Sanctity operates with religious fervor, hunting and executing AGC-carriers, believing they are performing a sacred duty to restore humanity to its "pure" state.  
  
**8. Faith vs. Science: The Great Debate**  
The true nature of the AGC remains a source of intense debate, a philosophical war waged between the worlds of science and magic.  
* **The GEB's Scientific View:** The GEB and its founder, Neuroxus, see the AGC as a biological puzzle to be solved. They believe that with enough research and experimentation, they can stabilize, replicate, and control any power, stripping it of its "mystical" origins and turning it into predictable technology.  
* **The Myst Witches' Magical View:** Descendants of Minka Myst, like **Aurelia Myst (The Butterfly Witch)**, believe the AGC is an extension of the curse and can only be understood through magic and ritual. They see the GEB's attempts to "scientize" it as an act of profound arrogance, doomed to fail and cause even greater chaos.  
* **UMED's Pragmatic View:** UMED remains agnostic, treating the AGC as a tangible threat to be contained, regardless of its origin. Their field agents are trained to handle both scientific and supernatural manifestations.  
Urban legends persist, further muddying the waters. Some whisper that Minka Myst is still alive, or that a second, even more potent Butterfly Curse is waiting to be unleashed upon the world.  
  
**9. Emotional Payload: The Human Cost**  
Beyond the science, politics, and magic, the AGC is, at its heart, a human tragedy. It is a story of bodies and lives irrevocably broken by a power they never asked for.  
**Redacted, Fleetview Clinic Log #2178:** *"The subject is a nine-year-old girl. Trauma-triggered activation occurred after she witnessed a domestic dispute. Her skin... it folded inside out. Her voice turned inward, collapsing into her throat. I have never heard a sound like it. We have her sedated, but something still looks at us through her eyes. I don't know if she's still in there."*  
**UMED Field Report - Dominion City Riot:** *"He just... came apart. One moment he was a man, the next he was a swarm of bone shards and screaming flesh. The crowd panicked. He didn't seem to be in control. He was just... dissolving. We found his wallet in the aftermath. His name was David. He had a picture of his kids."*
